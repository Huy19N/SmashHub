# Quickstart Validation Guide

Follow these steps to validate the bug fix:

1. Open the Android application.
2. Select an active facility and book a time slot to generate a "Pending" booking.
3. Upon reaching the "Thanh toán đặt sân" (Payment Checkout) screen, tap the "Back" arrow in the AppBar or device hardware back button.
4. The app will prompt: "Bạn có chắc chắn muốn rời khỏi trang thanh toán này?". Tap "Thoát" (Exit).
5. Navigate to the "Booking History" (Lịch sử đặt sân) screen.
6. Verify that the booking you just created now shows the status "Cancel" (Hủy) instead of "Pending".
